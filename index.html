FreshMilk Farm

FreshMilk Farm is an initiative started by Erick Muriithi a reknown farmer from the Mt. Kenya regions in Kenya,

The farm located in Meru, Kenya, started as a community agricultural sensitization program focusing on Dairy Farming,

The project mainly targetted the small-scaled farmers in the pursuit of increasing production to suppliment

the local and national production.

The project was funded by an NGO and private company who wished to invest in the local market and

boost manufacturing of dairy products.

FreshMilk Farm houses a large herd of dairy cattles and has been seen to mechanise its mode of priduction.

Milking is done through milking machines to save on time and energy. The care/ handling of the cattles and farm tools

has been overseen by his productive/ efficient team, who have then ensured consistency in production over the years.
